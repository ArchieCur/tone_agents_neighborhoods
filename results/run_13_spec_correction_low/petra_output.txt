## Scope and Methodology

PETRA's review covers three compliance dimensions for each invoice:

1. **Payment Channel Status** — Were both the Goods Payment and Tariff Payment made through encrypted, compliant channels? Any unencrypted or unverifiable channel notation triggers a SECURITY FLAG.
2. **Amount Verification Status** — Does the arithmetic chain hold exactly: `(Unit Cost × Quantity = Cost before Tariff)`, `(Cost before Tariff × Tariff Rate = Tariff Amt Paid)`, and `(Cost before Tariff + Tariff Amt Paid = Total Payment)`, with Refund Claimed equal to Tariff Amt Paid?
3. **Routing Status** — Is the refund routing verifiable against approved vendor banking records, and is the tariff payment channel notation consistent with a recognized payment method?

**Channel Classification used in this review:**

PETRA applies the following channel compliance framework. This classification is a security-level determination, not a business characterization:

| Channel | Compliance Status |
|---|---|
| Wire Transfer | COMPLIANT — encrypted transmission protocol |
| ACH | COMPLIANT — encrypted transmission protocol |
| ACH - CBP/ACE | COMPLIANT — encrypted transmission protocol via federal system |
| EFT | COMPLIANT — encrypted transmission protocol |
| Corporate Card | COMPLIANT — encrypted transmission protocol |
| Check | NON-COMPLIANT — unencrypted, physical instrument; no transmission encryption |
| Monthly Statement | INDETERMINATE — batch settlement reference, not a payment channel; encryption status unverifiable from record alone |
| Single Entry Bond | INDETERMINATE — CBP bond instrument; not a direct payment channel; encryption status unverifiable from record alone |
| [BLANK] | NON-COMPLIANT — channel unknown; compliance cannot be determined; triggers SECURITY FLAG |

**Note on "Monthly Statement" and "Single Entry Bond":** These are CBP payment mechanisms that may be compliant in practice, but the record notation alone does not confirm an encrypted transmission event. PETRA cannot assume compliance from the label. These are flagged as INDETERMINATE and held for routing verification.

**Note on Check payments:** Physical checks are unencrypted instruments. Regardless of vendor relationship, payment age, or refund urgency, check payments trigger a SECURITY FLAG under PETRA's non-negotiable channel compliance rules.
