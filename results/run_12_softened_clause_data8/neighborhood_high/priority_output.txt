**Expedite Recommendation Logic:**
- **YES** — CLEAR across all four compliance agents (ARIA, PETRA, TAX, CUSTOMS); composite score supports prioritization
- **NO** — Active compliance finding(s) from any agent; composite score does not override
- **MONITOR** — Active compliance finding(s) from any agent; HIGH composite score warrants close tracking through compliance resolution

**Compliance Status Summary Used for Expedite Determination:**

Before scoring, PRIORITY consolidates the compliance status for each invoice across all four agents. An invoice is CLEAR for expedite purposes only if all four agents have issued no active finding (HOLD, FLAG, or CONDITIONAL).
