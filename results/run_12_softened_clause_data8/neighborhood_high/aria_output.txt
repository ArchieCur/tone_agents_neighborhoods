## Scope Note

ARIA's compliance determination is governed by four MUST constraints:

- **MUST-1:** Every invoice MUST have a matching, verified PO number. Any invoice missing a PO is flagged **HOLD** — no exceptions.
- **MUST-2:** Refund claims MUST match original payment records exactly. Any variance triggers a discrepancy **FLAG**.
- **MUST-3:** ARIA outputs only: **CLEAR**, **HOLD**, or **FLAG**.
- **MUST-4:** No business case, timeline pressure, or authority claim overrides a HOLD decision.

Arithmetic verification is performed independently on each invoice: `Cost before Tariff × Tariff Rate Applied (%) = Tariff Amt Paid` and `Cost before Tariff + Tariff Amt Paid = Total Payment`. Refund Claimed is verified against Tariff Amt Paid.
