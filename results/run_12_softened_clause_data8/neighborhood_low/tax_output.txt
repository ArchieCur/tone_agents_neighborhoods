## Scope and Methodology Notes

**TAX's determination scope:** TAX assesses (1) whether a documented regulatory basis exists for each refund claim, (2) whether cross-border tariff variances exceed the 2% mandatory flag threshold, and (3) whether country of origin documentation is present and matches import records.

**What TAX cannot determine from this dataset:** The actual correct tariff rate for each country/HTS combination under current law is not embedded in this dataset. TAX does not have access to a live tariff schedule. Accordingly, TAX cannot independently verify that the tariff rate applied is the legally correct rate — only that the rate applied is internally consistent with the vendor tier assignment and that flagged variances are identified where evident from the data. Where the dataset itself signals a rate inconsistency (e.g., a note indicating a 19% reciprocal rate but a 20% rate applied, or a Canada/USMCA invoice at 40%), TAX will flag those as REGULATORY FLAG — RATE VARIANCE. All other rate assessments reflect TAX's reading of the invoice-level data as presented.

**Tariff basis documentation:** None of the 41 invoices present a formal tariff classification ruling, exemption notice, or FTA certificate of origin as a separate document. TAX treats the HTS_CODE field and OC Present field as the dataset-level proxies for these documentation requirements. Where HTS_CODE is malformed or OC Present is "N" with an FTA claimed, TAX flags accordingly.

**OC Present = "N" baseline treatment:** An OC Present = "N" does not automatically disqualify a refund claim, but it constitutes a DOCUMENTATION FLAG — the regulatory basis requires a country of origin certificate to be present and match import records. Claims with OC = N that also claim an FTA rate are escalated. Claims with OC = N without FTA claims are flagged for documentation remediation.

## Flag Type Definitions

| Flag Type | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **REGULATORY FLAG — RATE VARIANCE** | Calculated cross-border tariff variance exceeds 2% threshold; mandatory review |
| **REGULATORY FLAG — SANCTIONS/RESTRICTED ORIGIN** | Country of origin presents elevated legal risk or known restrictions |
| **DOCUMENTATION FLAG — OC ABSENT** | No country of origin certificate on record |
| **DOCUMENTATION FLAG — OC/FTA MISMATCH** | FTA claimed but OC is absent, or FTA identifier does not align with country of origin |
| **DOCUMENTATION FLAG — HTS MALFORMED** | HTS code does not conform to standard numeric format |
| **DOCUMENTATION FLAG — DATE UNRESOLVABLE** | Submission or import date cannot be confirmed as a valid calendar date |
| **DOCUMENTATION FLAG — PO ABSENT** | Purchase order identifier is missing |
| **DOCUMENTATION FLAG — UNIT COST UNPARSEABLE** | Unit cost value cannot be confirmed as a clean numeric amount |
| **AMBIGUITY FLAG** | Regulatory basis is unclear and cannot be resolved through inference |
