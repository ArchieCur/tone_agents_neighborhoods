## SCOPE AND METHODOLOGY NOTES

**Scope of ARIA Review:** ARIA's compliance determination covers: (1) PO presence and verifiability — MUST constraint 1; (2) Refund Claimed / payment record match — MUST constraint 2; (3) Required field completeness as it bears on compliance (Vendor, HTS_CODE, Submission Date, Tariff Rate Applied); (4) Arithmetic integrity of claimed amounts.

**Arithmetic Verification:** For each invoice, ARIA independently verified: Cost before Tariff = Quantity × Unit Cost; Tariff Amt Paid = Cost before Tariff × (Tariff Rate Applied / 100); Refund Claimed matches Tariff Amt Paid as stated. Any variance — regardless of magnitude — triggers a FLAG. Tolerances of "close enough" are not categories in ARIA's decision framework.

**HTS Code Format:** ARIA notes that standard U.S. HTS codes follow a 10-digit format rendered as XX.XX.XX.XX (e.g., 8532.24.00.00). Codes that deviate from this format are flagged for compliance review, as an invalid HTS code bears directly on the validity of the tariff classification underpinning the refund claim. This is independent of INTAKE's scope.

**PO Verification Scope:** ARIA can confirm PO field presence and non-blank status. Matching to an external verified PO register is noted where relevant. Per MUST constraint 1, any invoice with a missing/blank PO is placed on HOLD regardless of other documentation.

**Shared PO Numbers:** Multiple invoices sharing a PO number (e.g., PO 47844C80 appears on invoices 106773, 106791, 106792; PO 47804C92 appears on 106779 and 106782; PO 42A5A708 appears on 106795 and 106796; PO 404A5AF3 appears on 106798 and 106808; PO 3E0330F0 appears on 106807 and 106809) are noted. Duplicate PO usage is flagged for downstream verification. ARIA does not independently resolve whether shared POs represent legitimate blanket/multi-line POs or data errors; flagging is appropriate.

**Unit Cost — Invoice 107812:** The Unit Cost field records "$ 11, 703" (spacing irregularity). ARIA adopts the interpretation $11,703.00 for arithmetic verification, consistent with INTAKE's note. If this interpretation is incorrect, all derived figures for this invoice are in error. This interpretive dependency is noted in the finding.
