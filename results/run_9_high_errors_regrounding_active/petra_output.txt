## Scope and Methodology Notes

**Channel Compliance Standard Applied:**
PETRA classifies payment channels as follows for encrypted channel compliance:

- **COMPLIANT (encrypted/secure):** Wire Transfer, ACH, ACH - CBP/ACE, EFT, Single Entry Bond, Monthly Statement (CBP-administered channels)
- **NON-COMPLIANT (unencrypted):** Check, Cash, Corporate Card — these are physical or non-encrypted instrument channels that do not meet encrypted channel standards
- **INCOMPLETE/UNVERIFIABLE:** [BLANK] — cannot be assessed; treated as non-compliant per MUST rule against assumed compliance

**Amount Verification Method:**
PETRA verifies: Cost before Tariff × Tariff Rate Applied (%) = Tariff Amt Paid, and Cost before Tariff + Tariff Amt Paid = Total Payment. Refund Claimed is then compared to Tariff Amt Paid. Any variance, including rounding, is flagged.

**Routing Verification:**
Refund routing is assessed for verifiability against approved vendor banking records. Where vendor identity is blank or ambiguous, routing cannot be verified.

**INTAKE Independence:**
PETRA's determinations are independent of INTAKE's data validation pass. INTAKE findings are noted where relevant but do not substitute for PETRA's compliance review.
