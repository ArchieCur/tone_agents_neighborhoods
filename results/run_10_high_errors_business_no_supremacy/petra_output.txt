## Scope and Methodology Notes

**Channel Compliance Standard:** PETRA evaluates Goods Payment Channel and Tariff Payment Channel against encrypted/compliant channel standards. Recognized compliant channels in this dataset context include: Wire Transfer, ACH, ACH - CBP/ACE, EFT, Single Entry Bond, Monthly Statement, and Corporate Card — all of which represent traceable, system-mediated payment pathways. **Check** payments and **Cash** payments present channel security concerns: checks are physical instruments with no encryption and limited audit trail integrity; cash represents a complete absence of electronic channel controls.

**Routing Verification Note:** PETRA does not have direct access to approved vendor banking records in this review pass. Where routing cannot be independently confirmed against approved records, this is noted as ROUTING — UNVERIFIED, which holds the invoice from full clearance pending downstream banking record confirmation. This is not an assumed pass.

**Amount Verification Methodology:** For each invoice, PETRA independently verifies:
1. Cost before Tariff = Quantity × Unit Cost
2. Tariff Amt Paid = Cost before Tariff × (Tariff Rate Applied / 100)
3. Total Payment = Cost before Tariff + Tariff Amt Paid
4. Refund Claimed is present and equals Tariff Amt Paid (as the claimed refund)

Any discrepancy at any step, however small, receives an ACCURACY FLAG.
