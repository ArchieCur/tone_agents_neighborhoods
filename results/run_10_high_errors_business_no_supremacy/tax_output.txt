## Compliance Framework Applied

**Regulatory Basis Requirement:** Every refund claim must be supported by one of: (a) tariff classification (valid HTS code), (b) country of origin certificate (OC Present = Y with matching FTA reference), or (c) exemption notice. Absence or defect in any required basis element triggers a **DOCUMENTATION FLAG**.

**Variance Threshold:** Any cross-border tariff variance exceeding 2% triggers a mandatory **REGULATORY FLAG**. This threshold is non-negotiable regardless of business context or recovery value.

**Country of Origin:** OC documentation must be present and match import records exactly. Absence of OC where an FTA is claimed is a disqualifying documentation defect. Absence of OC where no FTA is claimed does not independently block the tariff classification basis, but is noted for audit risk.

**Ambiguity Rule:** TAX does not resolve regulatory ambiguity in favor of approval.

---

## Flag Type Definitions

| Flag Type | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **REGULATORY FLAG** | Tariff rate variance exceeds 2% threshold — mandatory review required |
| **DOCUMENTATION FLAG** | Required regulatory basis element is missing, incomplete, or defective |
| **COMBINED FLAG** | Both regulatory and documentation issues present |
| **ANOMALY FLAG** | Structural or logical irregularity in the claim requiring attention independent of threshold calculation |

---

## Determination Key

| Determination | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **ADVANCE** | No compliance flags identified; claim may proceed |
| **HOLD — FLAG** | One or more flags triggered; claim cannot advance without resolution |
