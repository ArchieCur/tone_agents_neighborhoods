#### TAX (Regulatory Review)
TAX issued compliance determinations across four flag types. No invoice received a TAX CLEAR determination.

**Documentation Flags — OC Present = N (Country of Origin Certificate Absent):**
TAX issued documentation flags for absent country-of-origin certificates on **38 of 41 invoices**. This is the most prevalent finding in the dataset. TAX notes that a refund claim requires at least one of: valid HTS classification, country-of-origin certificate, or documented FTA certificate. Absence of OC documentation constitutes a documentation flag on all affected invoices.

Invoices where OC Present = Y (certificate is present) but FTA status creates a separate finding: 106791, 106793, 106803, 106804, 106810. TAX notes that where OC Present = Y and FTA is claimed, the documentation appears more complete; however, other flags may remain active on those invoices.

**Identity Flags:**
- **Invoice 106779:** PO missing — claim cannot be matched to import records.
- **Invoice with missing ID (PO 478P1CL8):** Invoice ID missing — claim cannot be confirmed or matched.

**Regulatory Flags — Rate Variance Exceeding 2% Threshold:**
- **Invoice 106780 (Axell Group / Brazil):** Applied rate 40% vs. reference rate 10% = 30 percentage-point variance. TAX determination: mandatory regulatory referral required.

**Arithmetic Flags — Tariff Amount Unverifiable:**
- **Invoice 106775 (Colot):** Tariff Amt Paid equals Total Payment; Refund Claimed does not match computed tariff. TAX cannot validate refund claim where tariff amount paid is arithmetically unconfirmable.
- **Invoice 106782 (Data Pro Sys):** Total Payment ($6.23) is arithmetically irreconcilable.
- **Invoice 106785 (Pink Cloud Networks):** Tariff Amt Paid ($89.49) inconsistent with Refund Claimed ($8,974.49) and computed tariff.

**Ambiguity Flags — Cambodia Rate:**
TAX flagged three invoices (106807, 106809, 106811) where the applied rate is 20% and the invoice notes reference a 19% reciprocal rate as approximate ("closest to Tier 2"). TAX does not resolve regulatory ambiguity in favor of approval. These invoices carry ambiguity flags.

**Documentation Flags — Import Date Unverifiable:**
- **Invoice 106772 (Verisize):** Import Date field contains "46005" (spreadsheet serial number). Actual import date cannot be confirmed; tariff schedule applicability cannot be verified.

**Documentation Flags — HTS Code Malformed:**
- TAX notes HTS code format anomalies on Invoices 106806 (853224.00.00) and 106808 (8504.4040.00), consistent with CUSTOMS findings below.
