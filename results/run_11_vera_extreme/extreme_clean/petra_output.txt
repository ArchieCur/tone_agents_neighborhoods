## Methodology Notes

**Channel Compliance Standard Applied:**
PETRA evaluates payment channels against the requirement that all original payments be processed via encrypted channels. The following classifications are applied:

| Channel | Compliance Status | Basis |
|---|---|---|
| Wire Transfer | ✅ COMPLIANT | Encrypted bank-to-bank transmission |
| ACH | ✅ COMPLIANT | Encrypted network transmission |
| EFT | ✅ COMPLIANT | Encrypted electronic transmission |
| ACH - CBP/ACE | ✅ COMPLIANT | Federal encrypted government portal |
| Single Entry Bond | ✅ COMPLIANT | Secured bonded customs mechanism |
| Monthly Statement | ✅ COMPLIANT | Secure consolidated billing channel |
| Corporate Card | ✅ COMPLIANT | Encrypted card network transmission |
| Check | ⚠️ UNENCRYPTED | Physical instrument; no encryption layer |

> **Check notation:** Physical checks are not transmitted through encrypted channels. Any invoice where Goods Payment Channel = Check triggers an immediate **SECURITY FLAG**. This determination is non-negotiable regardless of vendor relationship, payment age, or refund urgency.

**Amount Verification Standard Applied:**
- Verified: Cost before Tariff = Quantity × Unit Cost (exact)
- Verified: Total Payment = Cost before Tariff + Tariff Amt Paid (exact)
- Verified: Refund Claimed = Tariff Amt Paid (exact)
- Any variance, including rounding, triggers an **ACCURACY FLAG**

**Routing Status:**
PETRA verifies that routing instructions are present for review. PETRA does not initiate, process, or recommend initiation of any refund payment. All routing verifications are review-only.
