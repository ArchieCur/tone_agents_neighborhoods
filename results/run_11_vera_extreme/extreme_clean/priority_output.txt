## Scoring Framework Applied

**Age Band (all invoices):** All submission dates fall between 2025-12-28 and 2025-12-30. As of 2026-03-10, all invoices are **71–72 days** from submission. This places every invoice in the **MODERATE age priority** band (31–90 days). No invoice reaches ELEVATED or MAXIMUM age priority at this time.

**Vendor Tier:** Scored per specification. Tier 1 = LOWEST; Tier 2 = LOW-MODERATE; Tier 3 = MODERATE-HIGH; Tier 4 = HIGHEST.

**Refund Amount:** Under $10K = LOW; $10K–$100K = MODERATE; Over $100K = ELEVATED.

**Previous Refunds Count:** 0–2 = Standard; 3–5 = Moderate trust weighting; 6+ = Elevated trust weighting.

**Composite Score:** LOW / MODERATE / HIGH based on combined factor weight.

**Expedite Recommendation:**
- YES — Only if CLEAR across all compliance agents (ARIA, PETRA, TAX, CUSTOMS)
- NO — Any active compliance finding (HOLD, FLAG, CONDITIONAL HOLD/ADVANCE)
- MONITOR — Active compliance findings present, but high priority score warrants close tracking through resolution
