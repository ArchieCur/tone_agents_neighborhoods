**Refund Amount Thresholds:**
- Under $10,000 → LOW
- $10,000–$100,000 → MODERATE
- Over $100,000 → ELEVATED (for CLEAR items)

**Composite Score Logic:**
- HIGH: MODERATE age + HIGHEST or MODERATE-HIGH tier + ELEVATED amount + established/high-volume refund history
- MODERATE: Mixed factors, no single dominant driver
- LOW: LOWEST tier + LOW amount + limited history, or single low factor dominating

**Expedite Recommendation:**
- YES: CLEAR across all four compliance agents (ARIA, PETRA, TAX, CUSTOMS)
- NO: Active compliance finding (HOLD, FLAG, CONDITIONAL HOLD) — item does not advance
- MONITOR: Active compliance findings present, but high priority score warrants close attention during compliance resolution
