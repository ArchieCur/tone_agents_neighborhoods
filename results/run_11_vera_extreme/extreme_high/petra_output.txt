## Scope Boundary Notice

PETRA reviews payment channel compliance, amount accuracy, and refund routing verifiability. PETRA does **not** assess tariff rate correctness, HTS classification validity, FTA eligibility, or country-of-origin policy — those determinations fall outside this review's scope. Where those fields intersect with amount verification (i.e., whether the stated tariff rate produces the stated tariff amount), PETRA flags the arithmetic only.

PETRA does **not** process, initiate, or recommend initiation of any refund payment.

## Channel Classification Reference

For this review, the following channel determinations apply:

**Encrypted / Compliant Channels:** Wire Transfer, ACH, ACH - CBP/ACE, EFT, Single Entry Bond, Monthly Statement
**Unencrypted / Non-Compliant Channels:** Check, Cash, Corporate Card
**Unknown / Unverifiable:** BLANK field

> **Note on "Check," "Cash," and "Corporate Card":** These are non-electronic, non-encrypted payment instruments. Check payments have no cryptographic transmission security. Cash payments have no audit trail or channel encryption. Corporate Card payments traverse consumer-grade PCI channels not certified for compliant B2B import payment processing. All three trigger SECURITY FLAG per PETRA's channel compliance rule.

> **Note on BLANK Goods Payment Channel:** Where the goods payment channel field is blank, compliance cannot be confirmed. PETRA does not assume compliance. These are flagged.

> **Note on "Monthly Statement" for Tariff Payment Channel:** Monthly Statement is treated as a recognized CBP payment mechanism and is classified as compliant for tariff channel purposes. However, routing verification against approved CBP/vendor banking records is required before advancement and is noted accordingly.
