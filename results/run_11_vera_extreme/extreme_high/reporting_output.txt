### TAX Findings Summary
TAX reviewed all 41 invoices. TAX issued a **HOLD on all 41 invoices.** The primary basis is described below.

**Universal Documentation Flag — OC Present = N (39 of 41 invoices):**
TAX has determined that country of origin certificates are absent for 39 of 41 invoices. No claim with OC Present = N can advance without remediation. This flag is applied as a HOLD basis across all 39 affected invoices. The two invoices where OC Present = Y (106791, 106793, and additionally 106803, 106804, 106810 per the dataset) carry separate compliance findings.

**Additional TAX findings by category:**

- **Regulatory rate authority absent:** TAX has determined that the specific regulatory citation supporting the applied tariff rate (Executive Order number, Federal Register citation, applicable Section 301 list) is absent from the record for the majority of invoices. This affects invoices across all origin countries in the dataset.
- **Section 301 rate citation absent (China-origin, Tier 4 invoices):** Invoices 106773, 106775, 106785, 106791, 106792, 106798, 106800 — specific Section 301 list and applicable rate citation absent.
- **USMCA/sectoral ambiguity (Canada-origin invoices):** Invoices 106777, 106779, 106782 — TAX has flagged that USMCA eligibility status is not resolved. If USMCA preference applies, the applied rate may be inapplicable; if a non-USMCA sectoral measure applies, the specific authority is required. Ambiguity is flagged, not resolved by inference.
- **Sanctions jurisdiction — REGULATORY FLAG:**
  - **Invoice 106772 (Libya):** Libya is subject to U.S. OFAC sanctions regimes. OFAC compliance verification is required before any refund determination can proceed. TAX HOLD issued on this basis in addition to OC absence and unverifiable import date.
  - **Invoice 106789 (Sudan):** Sudan sanctions/instability — elevated compliance exposure flagged by TAX. Regulatory review required.
- **FTA claims unsubstantiated without OC:**
  - **Invoice 106774 (Guatemala / GU43216):** CAFTA-DR FTA claimed; OC Present = N. FTA basis cannot be confirmed.
  - **Invoice 106784 (Peru / PE66297):** US-Peru TPA FTA claimed; OC Present = N. FTA basis cannot be confirmed.
- **Rate variance exceeding 2% threshold:**
  - **Invoice 106780 (Brazil):** Applied rate 40% vs. documented Tier 1 (10%) characterization — 30 percentage point variance. TAX has flagged this as a mandatory review item. No regulatory authority cited for the 40% rate.
  - **Invoice 106807 (Cambodia):** 1 percentage point variance noted (19% documented vs. 20% applied).
  - **Invoice 106809 (Cambodia):** 1 percentage point variance noted.
  - **Invoice 106811 (Cambodia):** Rate variance noted.
- **Arithmetic/field integrity findings (TAX):**
  - **Invoice 106775:** Total Payment ($438,964.02) equals Tariff Amt Paid as recorded — record integrity cannot be confirmed.
  - **Invoice 106782:** Total Payment ($6.23) is materially irreconcilable with Cost before Tariff ($55,282.00) and Tariff Amt Paid ($5,528.20). TAX has characterized this as a material data integrity failure.
- **Tariff rate field blank (Invoice 106779):** Rate cannot be validated; determination cannot proceed from inference.
